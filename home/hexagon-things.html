Claritas est etiam processus dynamicus, qui sequitur mutationem consuetudium lectorum. Mirum est notare quam littera gothica,quam nunc putamus parum claram anteposuerit.

Semi-Formal

Semi-formal is a dance traditionally held in early December. Students book tickets at a convention center rented by SAC for delicious food, quality music, polaroid pictures, and a night to remember!

Carnival

Carnival is a summer event filled with sunshine, food trucks, and bouncy castles. For two periods, students get a well-deserved break from exam stress by walking around and enjoying the events SAC has set up.

Graduation
Dessert

Grad desert is an event for all graduating students. It is a gathering topped with refreshments, a slideshow filled with photos, and even awards! We want to see all the students leaving TFSS off with a smile :)

Spirit
Weeks

Spirit week is designed to boost school spirit at Turner Fenton. Held on any week, SAC comes up with creative daily themes for students to follow, such as "anything but a backpack day", or "meme day".
